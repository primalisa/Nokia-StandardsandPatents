Standards and Patents

Turning technology innovation into growth

At Nokia, we believe that leadership starts with aligning one’s own interests with the greater good. In the case of technology leadership, the greater good can be achieved through highly interoperable, world-class, globally harmonized specifications. Our aim is to lead and drive the development of global standards with inventor-friendly policies that will generate significant value for the industry as a whole and sustainable growth opportunities for our company.

To achieve this aim, we relentlessly innovate, invent and create inclusive network technologies, systems and standards. This approach has led us to be a driving and trusted force for many standards-developing organizations, most notably 3GPP, the primary global standardization body for 5G.
